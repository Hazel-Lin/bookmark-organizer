# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and technically grounded collaboration.

Participants should:

- discuss ideas and evidence, not people
- assume good intent while being precise about risks
- keep feedback actionable
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or intimidation

## Unacceptable Behavior

- personal attacks
- abusive or demeaning language
- deliberate misrepresentation of test results or security impact
- sharing private user data without permission

## Maintainer Responsibility

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and discussions that harm the working environment or put users at risk.

## Reporting

Open a private security report for sensitive issues. For general conduct issues, contact the maintainer through the repository contact path once published.
